What are the positives and negatives of hosting your videos on YouTube?

- Embedding YouTube videos includes free hosting, SEO advantages, easy integration, cross-device compatibility, and potential monetization through ads. However, it also comes with drawbacks such as limited customization of the video player, potential distractions from YouTube's branding and related videos, reliance on YouTube's platform (which may remove or restrict videos), and concerns over data privacy. Additionally, YouTube’s server issues or platform changes can impact video availability, and you don’t have full control or ownership over your content.
