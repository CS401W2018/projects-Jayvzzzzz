And Still, He Made It

For 2 years a man waited

For a stamp and a note with confirmation

He didn’t quit, fueled with determination

2022, an email came

“Congratulations!”

Accepted, at last, to the dream he held tight

But before he could step through that door,

The consulate said “no”

Tears were shed, but he didn’t succumb

Life goes on, he did not give up

He got married, still having that dream in mind

To enter that university, and have a good life

He applied, but got denied

Tried two more times, and still got denied

“One last try” he said

The last try worked, and his dream was revived

That dream had a price, and time was running out

So he worked

Three jobs, one after the other

With side gigs in between and no time to cry

The weight was heavy, but the sacrifice would be worth it

Days passed by, he endured the trial

Finally, the time had come

A new chapter begins, and the dreams within reach

Now he stands with no regret in his eyes

With his wife by his side, their dreams intertwined

This is my story, still can’t believe this is real

Every step was worth it, every moment was real

My dream has been fulfilled

In the Home of the Brave, and the Land of the Free.
