What is normal flow in HTML?

- It is the default layout behavior in HTML. Elements are positioned one after another based on their natural order

What does float do? Why do you need to clear the float?

- Float pushes an element left or right. Clear stops the element from flowing around floated content.

What do absolute, fixed, and relative do?

Absolute - positions an element relative to its nearest positioned ancestor

Fixed - Positions an element relative to the browser window

Relative - Positions an element relative to its normal position in the flow.
