Jaimee Vicente

Jaimee Vicente is the only girl in the family, but don’t let that fool you—she’s as tough as they come. Confident, driven, and full of energy, she shines in everything she does, whether it’s dancing, joining school activities, or going head-to-head on the chessboard. Her recent win in a school chess competition is just one of many ways she proves that strength comes in all forms. Jaimee brings a unique spark to the family, balancing grace with grit and always stepping up to any challenge that comes her way.
