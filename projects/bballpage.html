My Favorite NBA Team and Basketball Player

Growing up, I really loved basketball. I'm not that good but I really enjoy playing it. Right now, my favorite team is the Golden State Warriors even though they are not really doing good this season, I still support them because I served in the bay area and that helped me to become a huge warriors fan. What also made me a fan of the warriors is the way they pass the ball around and how they are all really good 3pt shooters.

I love the warriors basketball team but my favorite player is not on their team. My favorite basketball playr that is still alive and playing today is Kawhi Leonard. He is my favorite player because he was the one that stopped Lebron James way back 2014. He is an excellent defender & a great scorer as well and that is why he is my favorite player right now. Kawhi is my favorite player right now but I have a list of my all time favorites as well like:

  1. Kobe Bryant
  2. Michael Jordan
  3. Tracy McGrady
  4. Vince Carter
