Why Basketball?

Growing up in the Philippines, I was exposed at an early age to basketball. My dad, my uncle, my cousins, and most of my friends play basketball. I learned how to play it and I loved it! I love it mainly because it is such a good feeling whenever I score a basket or when preventing someone from scoring.
